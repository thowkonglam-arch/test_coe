Test COE Mobilisation: Parallel to FPG's Transformation

8-Week Design to Operationalize. Quality Governance from Day One.

The COE runs in parallel to delivery — not after it. Structured across three phases from charter to continuous operation.

Weeks 1–3
Mobilise & Design Charter
  • TCOE leadership activated, WoW established
  • All 9 initiatives baselined against test standards
  • Initiative vendors onboarded and engaged
  • Critical-path test packages mapped
  • Test COE charter → ITMO governance aligned
Weeks 4–5
Design Against North Star
  • FPG-wide test standards & defect taxonomy defined
  • Test management tooling standardised across workstreams
  • Shift-left practices designed from BRD stage
  • Resiliency test items: DR, BCP, continuity scenarios
  • Regulatory checklist: PDPA, MAS TRM, PCI DSS, OWASP
  • North Star mapped to each of 9 initiatives
Weeks 6–8 → Ongoing
Operationalise & Continuously Improve
  • COE operations piloted across cross-programme workstreams
  • Test COE integrated into ITMO PSR reporting
  • Automation capabilities activated and expanded
  • COE refined continuously on live programme feedback
Guiding Principle: The Test COE is embedded from Day 1 — not a post-SIT safety net. Quality governance is in place before OneApp (May '26), before OneSAP (Oct '26), and through UWMS/HIVE/Products into 2027.
